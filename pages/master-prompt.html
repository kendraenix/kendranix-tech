Step 3 — Go Live

Deploy your page in 60 seconds

Once Claude gives you the HTML file, here's how to get it live on your own domain — no tech skills needed.

  • 1️⃣
    Save the fileClaude will output your complete HTML. Copy all of it and save it as a file called index.html on your computer.
  • 2️⃣
    Go to netlify.com/dropDrag and drop your index.html file directly onto the page. Netlify will give you a live URL in under 10 seconds. Free.
  • 3️⃣
    Connect your domain (optional)In Netlify → Site Settings → Domain Management, add your custom domain so it lives at something like links.yourdomain.com.
  • 4️⃣
    Update your bio linksSwap out your Linktree URL everywhere — Instagram bio, email signature, LinkedIn — with your new URL. You own it now.

Bonus — Domain Security

Lock down your website before you share this link everywhere

You just built a beautiful link page that lives on your domain. Now make sure that domain is actually secure. Here are the 3 most common ways mompreneur websites get hacked — and what to do about each one right now.

  • 🔓
    Weak or reused passwords on your domain registrarYour domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) is the master key to your entire online presence. Enable two-factor authentication TODAY and use a unique, strong password.
  • ⚠️
    Outdated WordPress pluginsOver 90% of WordPress hacks happen through outdated plugins. Set your plugins to auto-update, or audit them monthly and delete any you're not actively using.
  • 🚨
    No SSL certificate (http instead of https)If your site still shows "http" in the address bar, search engines flag it as unsafe. Most hosts offer free SSL through Let's Encrypt — turn it on in your hosting dashboard.
Real talk: If your domain or website gets hacked, you don't just lose your link page — you lose your email list, your SEO authority, and potentially your clients' trust. This is a $0 risk you can eliminate in 20 minutes. Do it today.
